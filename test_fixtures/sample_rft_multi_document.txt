NSW GOVERNMENT - COMMUNITY CENTRE REDEVELOPMENT
Request for Tender (RFT)

Project Reference: GOV-CC-2025-001
Closing Date: March 31, 2025

3. Project Methodology (MANDATORY)
   - Overall approach
   - Phase breakdown
   - Quality assurance processes
   - Risk management framework
   - Stakeholder engagement plan

4. Risk Register (MANDATORY)
   - Identified risks (minimum 10)
   - Mitigation strategies
   - Risk ownership
   - Likelihood and impact assessment
   - Contingency plans

5. Work Health & Safety Plan (MANDATORY)
   - Site safety procedures
   - Emergency response protocols
   - Training requirements
   - PPE requirements
   - Incident reporting procedures

6. Quality Management Plan (MANDATORY)
   - Quality standards
   - Inspection procedures
   - Documentation requirements
   - Non-conformance management
   - Continuous improvement

7. Environmental Management Plan (MANDATORY)
   - Environmental impact assessment
   - Mitigation measures
   - Compliance documentation
   - Waste management plan
   - Sustainability initiatives

8. Subcontractor Management Plan (MANDATORY)
   - List of proposed subcontractors
   - Capability statements
   - Management approach

9. Company Profile (MANDATORY)
   - Company overview

    - Public liability insurance

SECTION 2: TECHNICAL SPECIFICATION REQUIREMENTS

The Technical Specification must address:
- Construction approach using modular methodology
- Detailed timeline with monthly milestones spanning 12 months
- Resource allocation including labor (minimum 15 FTE), equipment, and materials
- Site constraints and access limitations management
- Coordination with existing facility operations
- Traffic management during construction
- Noise and dust control measures
- Community engagement during construction phase

SECTION 3: BILL OF QUANTITIES REQUIREMENTS

The Bill of Quantities must include:
- Itemized breakdown by construction phase
- Labor costs with skill categories (supervisors, skilled, unskilled)
- Materials with supplier details and lead times
- Equipment hire costs
- Subcontractor costs
- Preliminaries and general costs
- Contingency breakdown (minimum 5%)

SECTION 4: PROJECT METHODOLOGY REQUIREMENTS

The Project Methodology must describe:
- Overall project delivery framework
- Construction sequencing and logic
- Interface management between trades
- Quality assurance and testing protocols
- Progress reporting and governance
- Change management procedures
- Stakeholder communication plan
- Community consultation approach

SECTION 5: RISK REGISTER REQUIREMENTS

The Risk Register must contain:
- Minimum 10 identified risks across categories:
  * Construction risks
  * Environmental risks
  * Safety risks
  * Financial risks
  * Stakeholder risks
- Likelihood rating (1-5)
- Impact rating (1-5)
- Current controls
- Mitigation strategies
- Risk owner assignment
- Residual risk rating

SECTION 6: WORK HEALTH & SAFETY PLAN REQUIREMENTS

The WHS Plan must include:
- Site-specific hazard identification
- Safety management structure
- Induction and training programs
- Emergency evacuation procedures
- First aid facilities and trained personnel
- Incident investigation procedures
- Safety inspections and audits schedule
- Contractor and visitor management
- Personal protective equipment requirements

SECTION 7: QUALITY MANAGEMENT PLAN REQUIREMENTS

The Quality Management Plan must describe:
- Quality policy and objectives
- Quality organizational structure
- Inspection and test plans
- Hold points and witness points
- Non-conformance procedures
- Corrective and preventive actions
- Document control procedures
- Records management
- Continuous improvement processes

SECTION 8: ENVIRONMENTAL MANAGEMENT PLAN REQUIREMENTS

The Environmental Management Plan must address:
- Environmental policy commitment
- Environmental aspects and impacts register
- Legal and regulatory compliance
- Waste minimization and recycling
- Energy and water conservation
- Noise management
- Dust and air quality control
- Erosion and sediment control
- Flora and fauna protection
- Environmental incidents and emergency response

SECTION 9: SUBCONTRACTOR MANAGEMENT REQUIREMENTS

The Subcontractor Management Plan must include:
- List of all proposed subcontractors with:
  * Company details
  * Scope of work
  * Relevant experience
  * Insurance coverage
  * Safety record
- Subcontractor selection process
- Performance monitoring approach
- Payment terms and schedule
- Quality control measures
- Communication protocols

SECTION 10: COMPANY PROFILE REQUIREMENTS

The Company Profile must demonstrate:
- Company history and structure
- Financial stability (audited accounts last 3 years)
- Experience in similar projects (minimum 3 references)
- Current workload and capacity
- Key personnel qualifications and experience
- Organizational chart for this project
- Local presence and community involvement
- Innovation and technology capabilities

PROJECT SPECIFIC REQUIREMENTS

Budget: $5.2 million (GST exclusive)
Duration: 12 months from commencement
Location: 123 Main Street, Sydney NSW
Existing Operations: Facility to remain partially operational during construction
Heritage Considerations: Building is heritage listed, special care required
Community Consultation: Mandatory monthly community meetings

EVALUATION CRITERIA

Submissions will be evaluated on:
1. Technical Specification (25%)
2. Project Methodology (20%)
3. Price (Bill of Quantities) (25%)
4. Risk Management (10%)
5. WHS and Quality Plans (10%)
6. Company Experience and Capacity (10%)
